A princess party deserves hair that matches the occasion. These aren't everyday school styles — they're the hairstyles that appear in birthday photos your daughter will look back at for decades. Here are the most beautiful princess party hairstyles for South African little girls, all achievable without a professional.

The Flower Crown Style

Take the hair from the top of the head and sides and arrange it into a loose updo. Leave soft pieces framing the face. Instead of a literal crown, use a beautiful floral clip or rose clip at the crown of the head — the effect is princess-like and photographs beautifully without the practical challenges of a headband staying in place during an active birthday party.

The Braided Crown Accent

Take a small section from one temple and braid it across the top of the head, securing it behind the opposite ear with a beautiful clip. The braid creates a crown-like effect across the top of the head and elevates the whole look. The rest of the hair can fall freely in waves or curls, or be gathered in a loose low ponytail. Takes about five minutes and looks like it took twenty.

The Curly Princess

For children with naturally curly or wavy hair, a princess hairstyle doesn't need elaborate styling — it needs beautiful definition and one stunning accessory. Define the curls with a light product, gather the crown section and clip it to keep hair off the face, and let the rest fall in natural curls. A beautiful bow clip at the crown completes the royal look effortlessly.

The Cinderella Bun

A smooth, elegant bun at the nape of the neck with soft pieces left out to frame the face. Secure with elastics and finish with a large, beautiful clip or bow at the bun. This is the most polished and formal of the princess styles — beautiful for a seated birthday tea party or a dinner celebration. It requires a little patience but holds beautifully through a long birthday event.

SA Mom Party Tip

Do the birthday hairstyle before the dress goes on. This lets you use both hands freely, protects the outfit from stray product or water, and means any hair adjustment needed won't require undressing. Put on the dress last, take the birthday photos first.
